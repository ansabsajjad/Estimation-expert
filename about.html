ABOUT EXPERT ESTIMATION

Precision in Construction Cost Estimation Since 2010

Our Mission

At Expert Estimation, our mission is to provide the most accurate and reliable construction cost estimation services in the industry. We combine cutting-edge technology with deep industry expertise to deliver precise material takeoffs, quantity surveys, and cost analysis that help our clients make informed decisions, win more bids, and maximize their project profitability.

Our Estimation Expertise

Founded in 2010 by John Anderson, a professional estimator with over 20 years of experience in construction cost management, Expert Estimation was born from a simple idea: to provide construction professionals with the most accurate and reliable cost estimation services in the industry. What began as a small team of dedicated estimators has grown into a leading provider of construction cost consulting services.

Over the years, we've refined our estimation methodologies and embraced cutting-edge technologies to deliver precise material takeoffs, quantity surveys, and cost analysis. Our team of certified estimators brings together expertise in various construction sectors, including residential, commercial, industrial, and civil works.

Today, we've successfully completed over 5,000 estimation projects, helping contractors, developers, and architects make informed decisions and win more bids. Our commitment to accuracy, combined with our deep industry knowledge, has made us a trusted partner for construction professionals across the country.
