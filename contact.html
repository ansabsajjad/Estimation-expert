Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes, we provide free initial consultations and estimates for all projects. Our team will visit your site, discuss your requirements, and provide a detailed quote with no obligation.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve the greater New York metropolitan area, including all five boroughs, Long Island, Westchester, and parts of New Jersey and Connecticut. For larger projects, we may consider locations outside of this service area.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, Expert Estimation is fully licensed, bonded, and insured. We maintain all necessary certifications and insurance coverage to protect our clients and projects. Our license number is GC-12345.

How long does a typical construction project take?

Project timelines vary greatly depending on scope, complexity, and size. A small renovation might take 2-4 weeks, while a custom home could take 6-12 months. During our initial consultation, we'll provide you with a realistic timeline specific to your project.

Do you offer warranties on your work?

Yes, we stand behind our work with a comprehensive warranty. We offer a 1-year workmanship warranty on all projects, and many of the materials and products we use come with manufacturer warranties that may extend beyond that period.
